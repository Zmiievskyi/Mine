Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about mining with MineGNK

What is Gonka network?

Gonka is a decentralized AI inference network using Proof-of-Work. Miners earn GNK tokens by processing AI inference jobs with their GPUs.

How much can I earn?

Earnings depend on GPU type, network difficulty, and uptime. Check our pricing section for estimated daily rewards per GPU model.

Do I need my own hardware?

No! MineGNK provides fully managed GPU nodes hosted on Gcore Cloud infrastructure. You just monitor and collect rewards.

How long does setup take?

After you request nodes, our team provisions them within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email when your nodes are ready to start mining.

Can I scale up my mining operation?

Yes! You can request additional nodes at any time through your dashboard. There's no limit to the number of GPUs you can run.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency payments. Billing is monthly with no long-term contracts required.
